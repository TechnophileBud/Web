About us

Technophile Bud is a student community of ever-growing learners. We aim in working together as a team establishing trust, respect, and value for all its members. We’re the upcoming leading organization where we strive to build a community where students come together and shape themselves for a better future. Technophile Bud aims to guide the students at every walk of college life, right from choosing a college to the opportunities they can explore in the college. We are going to work under 4 broad domains:


1. Mentorship- Students tend to make mistakes mainly due to a lack of proper guidance. Our mentors provide valuable insights into career guidance, skill development, personality development, and CV enhancement.


2. Community Outreach- What you give, comes back to you. Serving the society not only adds to his/her CV but a person's personal growth as well. Therefore, we tie-up with big Organizations for our members to carry out volunteer work.


3. Internships- The most talked about yet confusing part of college. How to choose the right internship? We tend to connect you with us and for Internships in various domains like HR, Content Writing, Graphic Designing, Etc.


4. Networking- Acquiring skills is useful only if you have a good network to utilize them. We host regular meet-ups, events for our community members to broaden their network and connect them to well-established people.


Cracking your shell open and coming out of your comfort zone isn’t easy. Technophile Bud provides you a safe space for learning and falling without fear. You’re sure to come out as a confident, focused, and self-aware individual after joining Technophile Bud.
